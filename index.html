ISP Billing System – Seamless & Transparent Billing

Managing your internet billing has never been easier. Our ISP Billing System provides flexible plans, automated invoicing, and secure payment options to ensure smooth transactions for both providers and customers.

  • Flexible Billing Options – Choose from flat-rate, usage-based, or tiered pricing models.
  • Prepaid & Postpaid Plans – Select the best plan for your budget and usage.
  • Multiple Payment Methods – Pay via credit cards, bank transfers, or digital wallets.

Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our ISP Billing System, SMS Gateway, and M-Pesa & Bank Integration. Whether it's about billing, payments, or system integrations, we’ve got you covered with detailed solutions.

What is the ISP Billing System, and how does it work?

Our ISP Billing System automates the billing process for internet service providers, offering flexible pricing models, detailed invoicing, and multiple payment options.

Can I choose between prepaid and postpaid plans?

Yes, our system supports both prepaid and postpaid plans, allowing users to select the best option based on their needs.

What payment methods are supported?

We support credit/debit cards, bank transfers, digital wallets, and M-Pesa for seamless transactions.

How can I integrate the SMS Gateway with my system?

Our SMS Gateway provides API integration, allowing businesses to automate messaging, transactional alerts, and OTP verification.

Is the SMS Gateway suitable for bulk messaging?

Yes, our SMS Gateway enables businesses to send bulk SMS for marketing campaigns, notifications, and alerts with high delivery rates.

What security measures are in place for transactions?

We use end-to-end encryption, secure APIs, and comply with financial regulations to ensure safe transactions.
